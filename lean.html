Lean 4 formal verification

Key analytic lemmas supporting the amplitude envelope ansatz are proved without sorry in TribonacciDNLS.lean (this deposit) and TribonacciMeasure.lean (AXLE repository). The decay constant η ≈ 1.839287 is the Perron–Frobenius eigenvalue of the tribonacci companion matrix — the unique real root of x³ − x² − x − 1 = 0 in [1, 2].

η_characteristic : η³ = η² + η + 1✓ proved
w_pos : ∀ k, 0 < η⁻ᵏ✓ proved
